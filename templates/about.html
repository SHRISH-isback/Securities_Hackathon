About InsightGuard

InsightGuard is a tool designed to protect retail investors from fraudulent activities in the securities market. By leveraging real-time data and a sophisticated analysis engine, we provide a credibility score for corporate announcements, helping you make more informed investment decisions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote market integrity and build investor confidence. We are proud to be aligned with SEBI's Safe Space initiative, contributing to a safer and more transparent investment environment for everyone.

Disclaimer

The analysis provided by InsightGuard is for informational purposes only and should not be considered financial advice. Always conduct your own thorough research before making any investment decisions.
